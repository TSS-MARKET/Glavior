# GLAVIOR

AI-Powered Trading Intelligence Platform

GLAVIOR is an AI-native trading intelligence platform that transforms fragmented market data into actionable execution plans. Rather than displaying charts and indicators alone, GLAVIOR delivers institutional-grade trade intelligence through proprietary AI analysis.

## Overview

Financial markets generate enormous amounts of data every second, yet traders are still forced to manually combine technical analysis, market sentiment, on-chain information, and risk management before making decisions.

GLAVIOR solves this by providing complete trade intelligence generated by Pegasus, our proprietary AI analysis engine.

Each analysis includes:

- Market Direction
- Entry Price
- Stop Loss
- Take Profit
- Confidence Metrics
- Risk Assessment
- Supporting Intelligence

Every published trade is permanently recorded on a public immutable ledger, creating transparent and verifiable performance history.

# Core Features

## Pegasus Intelligence Engine

Pegasus is GLAVIOR's proprietary AI engine responsible for generating institutional-grade trade analysis.

It evaluates multiple market dimensions simultaneously before producing complete execution-ready trade intelligence.

Output includes:

- Direction
- Entry
- Stop Loss
- Take Profit
- Trade Confidence
- Risk Profile

---

## Public Immutable Ledger

Every published trade receives its own Trade ID and is stored on a public immutable ledger.

This creates:

- Transparent performance history
- Public verification
- Tamper-resistant records
- Complete accountability
